What it does

Transcript tools in one panel

  • Get available YouTube transcripts.
  • Search inside transcript rows while watching.
  • Export TXT, Markdown, or JSON.
  • Use playlist and channel workflows when available.

How to use it

Five quick steps

  1. Open a YouTube video.
  2. Click the ScribeDock extension.
  3. Open the side panel.
  4. Choose a transcript language.
  5. Copy or download the transcript.

Pro tips

Best results

  • Works best on videos with captions.
  • Some videos may not expose transcripts.
  • Use playlist or channel mode for bulk exports.
  • Scroll channel pages to load more visible videos.

Privacy and trust

Local by default

  • No account required.
  • No subscription required.
  • No transcript text sent to a ScribeDock server.
  • Local transcript cache stays in your browser to speed up reloads.
